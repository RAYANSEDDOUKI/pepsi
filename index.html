Pepsi is a carbonated soft drink produced by PepsiCo, originally created by Caleb Bradham in 1893 as "Brad's Drink" and renamed Pepsi-Cola in 1898. It is known for its sweet, caramel-like flavor with a hint of citrus. Pepsi competes primarily with Coca-Cola and has various versions like Diet Pepsi and Pepsi Max. PepsiCo also produces a wide range of other beverages and snacks.
